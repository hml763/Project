Our Work

Capstone Health Measuring Company is here to provide you a multiple diagnosis of your body health. Our system is based on Capacitive sensing, we use that body sensing machanism to detect human 's ECG; Heart activity, and ECG; Muscle activity. Our research was being helped by CNU, Cheonnam National University of Korea, an ITSOC LAB, from Prof.YoungCheol Kim, and Prof.Cheolhong Kim. For more detail, we post our website below. Our main purpose of this study is to enhance our comprehension of the body-sensing, in consequence, we give this human-body-sensing-device.
